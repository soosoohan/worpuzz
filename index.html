This is a single-player WORPUZZ game where you take turns with the computer clicking grid cells to find hidden words.
Words are hidden horizontally, vertically, and diagonally. You win a word by uncovering its last letter.
The goal is to find more words, and the game ends without winners or losers.

🍀 Opening a clover reveals 4 adjacent cells and gives you an extra turn.
🌼 Opening a daisy reveals all 8 surrounding cells and also gives you an extra turn.
Choose your preferred word pool below to start the game.
